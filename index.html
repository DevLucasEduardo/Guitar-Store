Les Paul

The Les Paul is an iconic electric guitar designed by Les Paul himself. Known for its solid body construction and warm, powerful tone, it's a favorite among rock and blues guitarists. The Les Paul's unique shape, luxurious finishes, and versatile pickups make it a timeless choice for musicians.

Stratocaster

The Stratocaster, or "Strat," is a legendary electric guitar by Leo Fender. With its comfortable contours and double-cutaway body, it revolutionized guitar design. The Stratocaster's three single-coil pickups offer a bright, twangy sound, making it versatile for various genres like rock, blues, and funk.

SG

The SG, short for "Solid Guitar," is a lightweight, sleek instrument from Gibson. Popular among rock and metal guitarists, the SG's mahogany body and dual humbucking pickups deliver a biting tone with plenty of sustain. Its double-cutaway design allows for easy access to the upper frets, perfect for expressive playing.
